A Hidden gem for good coffee & good company

Why people love odd socks

More than just a cafe, Odd Socks is a relaxed, dog-friendly, wheel-chair accessible coffee shop in Cirencester where people come together. Enjoy fresh coffee, breakfast and lunch with free Wi-Fi, join our mums & baby groups, browse the book swap, or come along to one of our regular events and evening openings.

Where To Find Us?

Just outside Cirencester town centre, we're a quiet, relaxed spot with free parking and outdoor seating, perfect for escaping the busy high street.
